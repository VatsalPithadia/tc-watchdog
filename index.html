What We Detect

What We Flag For You

We classify every clause into three clear categories.

🔴 Danger

  • Selling your personal data
  • Forced arbitration clauses
  • Waiving right to sue
  • Hidden auto-renewal charges
  • Unlimited data collection

🟡 Warning

  • Sharing data with partners
  • No refund policies
  • Account suspension rights
  • Terms can change anytime
  • Content ownership claims

🟢 Safe

  • Standard cookie usage
  • Basic account terms
  • Normal service agreements
  • Standard privacy practices
  • Regular billing terms
